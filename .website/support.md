# Support Us

Serinus is a MIT licensed open-source project maintained by a small team of developers. Your support helps us continue to improve and maintain the framework.

## Ways to Support

Serinus doesn't have a big corporate sponsor, so we fully rely on the community to help us keep the project alive. But we want to keep improving Serinus, making it better and better for everyone. If you want to support the project, here are some ways you can help:

- **Star the repository on GitHub**: This is a simple way to show your support and help others discover the project.
- **Github Sponsors**: You can become a sponsor on [GitHub Sponsors](https://github.com/sponsors/francescovallone). Your sponsorship will help us dedicate more time to the project and improve it faster.
- **Direct donation on PayPal**: You can also support us with a direct donation via [PayPal](https://paypal.me/francescovll). Every contribution, big or small, helps us keep the project going.

## Thank You

We want to thank everyone who has supported Serinus so far. Your contributions, whether through code, documentation, or financial support, have been invaluable in helping us build and maintain this project. Thank you for being part of the Serinus community!
